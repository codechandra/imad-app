Instructions to the returning officer



1 . Make sure that the system in which online voting is conducted is in a good position.
2 . Before allowing the voter to vote check all his credentials.
3 . Make sure that each voter casts only one vote.
4 . If any voter violates the election code abandon his vote of power
5 . Make sure that the election starts and ends as per the scheduled timings
6 . If prolongation of the timing is required,make sure that you get specified orders from district collector.
7 . Let's hope that everything goes well.JAI HIND
